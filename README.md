Car Driving Simulation <br />
A basic car driving simulation with real car driving logic made by using pygame <br />

Select your car <br />
Press -- <br />
    c           --- clutch  <br />
    1,2,3,4,5,6 --- 6 gears   (with clutch)  <br />
    down arrow  --- brake  <br />
    u parrow    --- accelerator  <br />
    c + enter   --- engine on <br />
